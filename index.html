SINGLE PLAYER

  • Goal: Eat food to grow and score points.
  • Avoid crashing into walls or your own tail.
  • Use power-ups to survive longer or score higher.
  • Warning: Mines are deadly obstacles. Don't crash!
  • Ghost Mode: Allows you to pass through mines/walls.

2-PLAYER (COMBAT MODE)

  • Goal: Outlive your opponent or kill them.
  • Head-on Collision: Face-to-face crash = Mutual destruction (unless shielded).
  • Side Collision: Crashing into the enemy's body kills YOU.
  • Shield Smash: If you are shielded, you can ram an unshielded enemy to kill them without dying.
  • Mines: Mine power-ups drop Mines. Mines are safe for the owner (you can pass through them) but deadly for the enemy!
